Improvement in the use of price lists

On November 2 by Federico

When we added price lists in myAbakus, we did it to facilitate the process of updating prices and to provide the option of having two prices for any product or service (for example, retail and wholesale), so that our users could select the correct one to use (on new transactions) as part of a customerâ€™s record.

But what happens if I want to use both prices for the same customer?

Now I can do it by selecting the appropriate one in new transactions. So even if the customer has a price list assigned, which will determine the initial price selection, I can also select the other price or simply enter a new one.

Note: If you arrive at the Price field using the keyboard (instead of the mouse), you can press de letter P to see the prices you can select.
